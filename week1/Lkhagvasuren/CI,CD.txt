GitLab CI/CD (Continuous Delivery гэж юу вэ?)

CI buyu continuous integration bol automataar testing hih ym. 

CD buyu continuoys delivery gedeg ni CI daraa hereglegch nariin gart shuud hureh automatjuulalt ym.

hunii oroltsoo baihgui CD bol continuous deployment. Hogjuulegch codeoo push hiile. automataar CI daraa ni CD.

Gitlab deer CI deer negjiin test tohiruulsan node.js-tei CD (delivery) hiij boldog.


Gitlab CI huvid:
build,test hiij bdg.
1. ICS hereglegch, 2 VM, Gitlab CE
2. Gitlab runner on VM
3. GitLab deer project uusgeh

Gitlab CD huvid:
packagelana gesen ug.
1. Shine VM uusgeh
2. Shine deploy stage nemne
